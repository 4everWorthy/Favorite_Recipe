Step by step directions to make Cheesy Mac & Cheese

Instructions:

Steps:

  1. Boil noodles for 8 minutes, strain, then one rinse with warm water and strain again.
  2. Add butter and noodles to pan (heat from noodles should melt the butter), add salt and pepper then mix together until well mixed. Note: Make sure butter has melted and taste for desired flavor
  3. Wisk eggs together then add to pan along with the cheese soup and evaporated milk and mix again.
  4. Then add your Sharpe and Colby cheese. Cut the cream cheese into small cubes then add to mixture and mix everything together until well mixed.
  5. Cover the entire surface with Mozzarella cheese, then bake uncovered for 45 minutes to 1 hour or until you form a crust of your liking.
