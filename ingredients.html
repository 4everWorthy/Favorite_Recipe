Ingredients to make Cheesy Mac & Cheese

Feeds 15 - 20

  • 1 lb (16oz) elbow noodles
  • 1 1/2 sticks of unsalted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 8oz block of cream cheese
  • 24oz (1.5lb) Shredded Mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of pepper
  • 10oz Campbell's cheese soup
  • 12oz Evaporated milk
